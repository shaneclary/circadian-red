Why This Matters

üß¨ Melanopsin Biology

Your eyes contain melanopsin photoreceptors with peak sensitivity at 479nm. Exposure to 446-477nm blue light suppresses melatonin with devastating efficiency - requiring only 30 lux for measurable disruption.

üíß Structured Water

Gerald Pollack's research shows 450-500nm blue light actively destroys cellular structured water (EZ water), while red and infrared wavelengths enhance it. The same wavelengths disrupting sleep also damage cellular health.

üî¥ Red Light Safety

Wavelengths above 620nm show <10% melanopsin activation. Studies show 631nm red allows melatonin recovery after 2 hours, while blue maintains suppression. CircadianRed targets <3 lux intensity for maximum safety.
